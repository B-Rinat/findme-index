    • About

    “Findme” application further simply app or “Findme” strive to achieve following aim. Allow users to quickly create tags on the map and share current location between users in real time which is really helpful when users want to see each other’s location on the map and follow in the direction towards each other, for example, to meet a friend in unexplored area, track your school kid movement from home to school and back, and many more other possibilities what you can do with this app. Moreover, this application provide you with up-to-date information about how far users are located from each other.

    Registration

    The registration process would be no difficulty. Thankfully, the need for app passwords is dwindling with the passage of time. Looking at such giants like Facebook, Twitter, Whatsapp, Telegram, Tumblr which have functionality of registering users levering their phone numbers. “Findme” in that regard isn’t an exception. The only thing you need before installing application is working phone number. After downloading and installing app from Play Store you need to enter your phone number in international format, like [country code] [phone number] without plus sign. Below are some examples.



    Next step is to confirm your phone number you entered earlier. Confirmation screen will pop up and you need to type a confirmation code which should arrive by SMS after some time. Usually SMS arrives from 3 seconds to 2 minutes.

    How to track person’s location?

    When you successfully registered in the app, (if you haven’t done so please see Registration), the next step is to add new contact who already registered in the “Findme”. See below on how to do it.

    Adding new contact into app

    If both users were already registered in the app, near to added contact should appear locator icon as shown by red arrow on an image below.

    Initiating meeting

    Now it’s time to initiate new meeting with user using locator icon. Press that icon and wait until invitation will be send to invited person and he/she will accept your invitation. Once person accepted/rejected your invitation you should receive push-notification informing about invitation acceptance or its rejection to share person’s location with inviter of the meeting. On the other hand, person who was invited receives push-notification from initiator of the meeting.Below is an example of how users will see each other’s location on the map.
